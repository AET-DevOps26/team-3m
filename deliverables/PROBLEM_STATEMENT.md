# PROBLEM STATEMENT

„Finanzfluss“ Copilot about financial topics (Personal Expenses, Wealth, Stocks) and AI Support (Expense Optimization, intelligent Portfolio Reallocation), RAG? Connection with Stock Data (Yahoo Finance)

- What is the main functionality?

Personal Wealth Management and Expense Tracking Progressive Web App (Mobile, WebApp) with GenAI support for personalized financial insights and recommendations.

- Who are the intended users?

Young professionals, investors, and anyone interested in managing their personal finances effectively.

- How will you integrate GenAI meaningfully?

GenAI will be integrated to analyze users' financial data and provide personalized insights, such as identifying spending patterns, suggesting ways to optimize expenses, and recommending portfolio reallocations based on market trends and individual risk tolerance. The insights engine will scan news and financial data to provide real-time recommendations and alerts.

- Describe some scenarios how your app will function?

## Requirements

### EPIC: Personal Portfolio Management

- CSV Import of Transactions (Expenses, Income, Investments)
- Portfolio Overview (Cash, Stocks, Crypto, etc.) with current value per asset and total value
- Show historical performance of the portfolio and individual assets
- Show key metrics like returns, volatility, IRR, etc.
- show Portfolio Allocation (country, sector, etc.)
- Performance Comparison with benchmarks (e.g., S&P 500)
- Dividend calendar with upcoming dividends and historical dividend payments

### EPIC: Financial News and Research

- Watchlist with search and add functionality for stocks, ETFs, cryptocurrencies, etc.
- Price alerts for assets in the watchlist
- Information about stocks, ETFs, cryptocurrencies, etc. (current price, historical performance, management fees, etc.)
- Comparison of assets (e.g., compare two stocks or ETFs side by side) with AI support for insights on differences and potential advantages/disadvantages of each asset

### EPIC: Personal Finance Advisor (AI-driven)

- Provide personalized financial insights based on the user's portfolio and transaction history
- Recommend portfolio adjustments based on market trends and individual risk tolerance
- Based on news, provide real-time recommendations for portfolio adjustments

### EPIC: Tax Optimization and Reporting

- Reminders about "Quellensteuer" for dividends and capital gains
- Freistellungsauftrag management and tracking
- Vorabpauschalen Reminders and tracking
- Wash Sale advisory to identify potential tax optimization opportunities (with legal disclaimers in respective countries)

### EPIC: Cash Flow and Expense Tracking

- CSV Import of Transactions (Expenses, Income, Investments)
- Expense Categories and Budgeting
- Visualize Spending Patterns and Trends
- Provide Insights on Expense Optimization (AI-driven)
